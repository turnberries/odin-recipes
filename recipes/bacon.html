Bacon

Description

What more need I say? Let's cook everyone's favorite food. Bacon

Ingredients

  • 16 slices thick cut bacon
  • Ground red pepper flakes

Get Cookin

  1. Pre heat oven fo 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Arrange bacon an sheet pan
  3. Sprinkle red pepper flakes to taste
  4. Heat of 20 mins or until desired crisp
  5. Serve hot
