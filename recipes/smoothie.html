Blueberry Smoothie

Description

Some mornings suck. And for those mornings I find that nothing gets rid of the suck like a nice refreshing smoothie. A favorite of mine is blueberry, but this can be modified to use whatever ingredients you want.

Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 8 oz, plain greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • a dash on nutmeg

Get Cookin

  1. Blend ingredients to desired constistency. Use spoon to clean down sides of blender as needed
  2. Serve right away
