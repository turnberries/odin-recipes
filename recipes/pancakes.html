Pancakes

Description

Who doesn't love a lovely stack of flap jack? I know I do that is why I'm sharing my favorite method of making them, here, with yu folks!

This is a quick and I mean quick 7 ingredient recipe that your sure to lover. So buckle up, grab your preferred chef's hat and lets take a look below to see what we need.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flower
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • Something to coat your pan with

Get Cookin'

  1. Mix ingredients in bowl until smooth
  2. Heat your skillet and coat with whatever you selected
  3. Pour or scoop batter into pan, using about 1/4 cup at a time
  4. Heat untill brown on each side
  5. SERVE HOT
